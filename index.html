Skill Development

Skill development is a lifelong journey that requires dedication, curiosity, and a willingness to step out of one's comfort zone. Enterpreneur

Entrepreneurship is the process of identifying opportunities, innovating, and taking risks to create new ventures or businesses. Entrepreneurs are individuals who initiate and manage these ventures, often with the goal of generating profits or creating social impact.Entrepreneurship plays a vital role in driving economic growth, innovation, and job creation,etc.

Information Technology

Science graduates with an interest in technology can pursue careers in IT fields such as software development, cybersecurity, data analysis, or network administration. They design and develop software applications, secure computer systems, analyze data to extract insights, or manage IT infrastructure for organizations.

Legal Studies

Legal Studies is an interdisciplinary, liberal arts major that engages the meanings, values, practices, and institutions of law and legality.Legal Studies provide a general understanding of legal systems, how regulations are formed and which are the main stakeholders involved in the legal process.
